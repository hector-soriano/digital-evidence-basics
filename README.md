# Digital Evidence Basics

This repository contains a concise technical overview of fundamental concepts related to digital evidence and its treatment in legal and investigative contexts.

The content is intended for educational and reference purposes, focusing on the technical principles that govern the identification, preservation, analysis, and documentation of digital information that may be relevant in legal proceedings.

## Scope

The material covered includes:

- Basic concepts of digital evidence
- Principles of integrity and traceability
- Chain of custody considerations
- Technical documentation of digital findings
- Overview of common digital environments (systems, networks, and data storage)
- Neutral and objective analysis principles

## Purpose

The purpose of this repository is to provide a high-level technical reference that emphasizes rigor, neutrality, and alignment with legal standards when dealing with digital information.

No offensive security techniques, exploitation methods, or operational tools are included.
